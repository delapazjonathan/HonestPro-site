How your credits work

We keep your credit system simple and transparent so you always know what you’re paying for.

  • Each lead costs a set number of credits based on category and job size.
  • Credits are only used when a lead is actually sent to you.
  • We never charge extra just to contact a customer you already matched with.
  • If a lead is clearly bad (wrong number, spam, obviously outside your services), you can request a review for potential credit return.
