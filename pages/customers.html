Why customers use HonestPro

If you’ve ever filled out a form online and then regretted it, HonestPro is built to feel different. We focus on fewer pros, clearer choices, and less pressure.

No spam or sales blitz

We don’t sell or blast your information to a long list of strangers. Only a small group of relevant pros sees your request.

  • No endless unknown numbers calling you.
  • No “who even is this?” messages at 10pm.
  • You decide how and when to respond.

Up to 3 serious options

Instead of sorting through 15–20 pros, we focus on a smaller pool that actually fits your project, budget, and location.

  • Local to your area whenever possible.
  • Matched to the type of work you need.
  • Clear, comparable quotes.

You stay in control

Your contact details stay private until you’re ready to move forward. You choose who to work with — or decide not to hire anyone at all.

  • No obligation to hire.
  • Close or update your request anytime.
  • Block pros you don’t want to hear from again.

What to expect after you submit a request

  1. You’ll see a confirmation screen. We’ll show you that your request was received and what happens next.
  2. We quietly match you behind the scenes. We look at your location, type of work, and timing to find good fits.
  3. Up to 3 pros reach out. They may message, call, or send a quote depending on what you choose.
  4. You compare and decide. Ask questions, look at quotes, and go with the pro you feel good about — or decide not to move forward.

As we roll out the customer dashboard, you’ll also be able to log in to see your active and past requests in one place.
