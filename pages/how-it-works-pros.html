How it works for pros

Fair, predictable leads that respect your time and budget.

HonestPro is designed so you always know what you’re paying, how many pros you’re competing with, and what you’re getting in return. No mystery fees, no “who else got this lead?” guesswork.

Apply as a Pro View pricing

You focus on doing good work. We focus on sending you jobs that actually make sense for your business.

Quick snapshot

From your side, the system looks like this:

  • You set up your profile and load credits.
  • You get matched to jobs that fit what you do.
  • You track leads, jobs, and credit usage over time.

The 3 steps pros follow

We keep your side of the process as straightforward as possible: clear setup, clear leads, clear results.

1

Set up your profile

Tell us what services you offer, where you work, and what types of jobs you’re actually looking for — not just “everything.”

  • Choose your service categories.
  • Set your service area or radius.
  • Add basic business details and contact info.
2

Load credits to unlock leads

You buy credits up front so you’re ready when a good job appears. Each lead uses a set number of credits based on the job type.

  • See lead pricing clearly before you commit.
  • No surprise “extra” fees after the fact.
  • Pause or scale your spend as your schedule changes.
3

Get matched to real jobs

When a customer request fits your services and area, we send it to you and a small group of other pros — not a giant list.

  • Only a limited number of pros see each lead.
  • Enough competition to stay fair, not so much that it’s chaos.
  • You decide how quickly and how strongly to pursue each job.

What a typical lead looks like from your side

You’re not just getting a name and a phone number. You’ll see the key details you need to decide if the job is worth your time.

Job details you’ll see

  • Type of work (painting, cleaning, detailing, etc.).
  • Location and basic timing.
  • Customer notes about the project.

How you respond

  • Send a quick intro and ballpark estimate.
  • Ask clarifying questions before giving a final quote.
  • Politely decline jobs that aren’t a fit.

How credits, fairness, and protection work

We know you’re not just chasing “leads” — you’re protecting your time and budget. The credit system is there to keep things balanced.

Credits instead of surprise charges

  • You load credits in advance, on your own terms.
  • Each lead costs a clear, fixed number of credits.
  • No extra per-lead fees tacked on after the fact.

Protection against bad or unfair leads

  • We work to filter out obvious junk or fake requests.
  • Leads that clearly don’t meet our standards can qualify for review.
  • As we launch, we’ll refine the rules with early pros like you.

Where this is headed: your Pro Dashboard

As we roll things out, your Pro Dashboard will become the place you manage everything: leads, credits, and performance over time.

  • See new and active leads at a glance.
  • Track which jobs you’ve won and from where.
  • Review how your credits are being used.

During the early launch, some of this will be simple and we’ll be in closer contact with you. The goal is to build the right tools alongside real pros, not just in theory.
