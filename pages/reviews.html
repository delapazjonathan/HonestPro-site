Reviews

What customers and pros say about HonestPro.

We’re in early stages, but the goal is simple: fewer headaches, more clarity, and a lead system that feels fair for both sides. Here’s a preview of how reviews and feedback will show up as we grow.

Find a pro Apply as a pro

These examples are based on the kind of feedback we’re building toward. As real reviews come in, this page will shift from sample stories to real-world results.

Snapshot (goal)

  • Clear, honest reviews from customers.
  • Pros rated on quality, not just price.
  • Feedback used to improve matches over time.

Early feedback we’re building around

As HonestPro launches, we’ll invite both customers and pros to share what worked well and what we can improve. The goal isn’t to cherry-pick only perfect reviews, but to show real experiences.

Customers

“Less noise, clearer choices.”

Customers tell us they want a few strong options instead of a long list. HonestPro focuses on sending you up to a handful of pros that actually fit what you asked for.

Pros

“Real jobs, better expectations.”

Pros care less about sheer volume and more about real customers with clear project details. The credit system and smaller competition pool are built around that.

How reviews will work as we grow

Reviews aren’t just for show — they help customers choose, and they help pros see what’s working. Here’s the direction we’re heading.

For customers

  • Leave a review after a job is completed.
  • Share a short summary, rating, and photos (optional).
  • Highlight pros you’d happily hire again.

For pros

  • Showcase consistent good work over time.
  • Respond to feedback and improve your profile.
  • Stand out in matching when your reviews are strong.
