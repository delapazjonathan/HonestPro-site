Privacy Policy

How HonestPro protects your information

This is a placeholder privacy policy for demo purposes only. When you’re ready to go live, we’ll replace this with a real policy drafted by an attorney or a trusted template.

1. Information we collect

HonestPro collects information you provide directly to us, such as your name, email address, phone number, project details, business details, and any messages you send through the platform.

  • Customer account details and project requests.
  • Pro / business account details and service offerings.
  • Messages and interactions between customers and pros.
  • Basic usage data, such as pages visited and actions taken.

2. How we use your information

We use your information to connect customers and pros, improve our services, and keep the platform running smoothly and securely.

  • To match customers with relevant pros and local businesses.
  • To send notifications about new leads, messages, and updates.
  • To monitor platform performance and prevent fraud or misuse.
  • To communicate with you about your account or our services.

3. How we share (and don’t share) data

HonestPro is built around a simple idea: we only share information when it helps customers and pros work together, not to flood anyone with spam.

  • We share customer project details with up to 3 matched pros.
  • We share pro contact and profile details with interested customers.
  • We do not sell your personal information to random third parties for unrelated marketing.

4. Data security & retention

We use industry-standard measures to help protect your account and data. No system is perfect, but we design HonestPro to minimize unnecessary data collection and exposure.

5. Your choices

You can update or delete certain information from your account, control notification settings, and ask us questions about how we handle your data.
